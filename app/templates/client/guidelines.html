Event Setup Guidelines

Please review these guidelines before your event to keep the setup smooth, safe, and on schedule.

  1. Props are for rental use only. Please do not take any items home after the event.
  2. Handle all props with care. Damaged items may incur additional charges.
  3. No decor changes after setup. Once decor and balloons are arranged, we follow the agreed mock-up to achieve the planned design.
  4. Backdrop location is fixed. Please keep it in the agreed spot after it has been set up.
  5. Allow the stylist to work freely. To maintain design quality, please avoid adjusting or interfering with the setup process.
  6. If guests arrive early: We appreciate your patience in giving our team the time and space to finish the backdrop without distractions.
  7. Setup time starts after ingress. Bringing in materials is separate from setup time. Timing starts once all materials are inside the venue.
  8. Setup duration: A basic backdrop requires at least 2 hours. More elaborate designs may take longer depending on complexity.
